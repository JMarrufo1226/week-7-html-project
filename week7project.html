What can you do here?

Great question! In this town, based on the same location from one of my favorite video games, you can do many things that will help you on your explorations. You can:

  • Buy Items at Keckleon Market
  • Store Items at Kangaskhan Storage
  • Learn Moves at Elective Link Shop
  • Deposit/Withdraw Money at Duskull bank
  • Drop Off Eggs at Chansey Day Care
  • Train at Marowak Dojo
  • And Unlock Boxes at Xatu Appraisal

It seems some shops are closed at the moment... But the basic necessities are open, so feel free to prepare before you head out on your next adventure. Have fun!
